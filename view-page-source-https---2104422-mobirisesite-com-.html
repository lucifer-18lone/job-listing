Got Questions, We Got Answers

01

How do I sign up?

Just hit that sign-up button, pick your role (seeker or employer), and follow the ridiculously simple steps. No rocket science degree required.

02

What's with the JWT?

It's like a secret handshake for your login, keeping things secure and speedy. We're not messing around with your data, unlike some other places.

03

Can I filter jobs?

Absolutely! Filter by keywords, location, salary, and job type. We've made it easy to find exactly what your wallet and your sanity crave.

Easy peasy. Log in, go to your employer dashboard, and fill out the form. Title, description, salary the whole shebang. Make it sound amazing!

Job seekers can upload their resumes directly to their profile. Employers can then view these digital masterpieces when reviewing applications. Impress them!
